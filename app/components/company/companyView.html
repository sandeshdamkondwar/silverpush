About Us

SilverPush is a leading Cross-device Mapping platform that unify data points from the billions of digital devices around the globe and associate it with people.

We combine our patent-pending audio beacon technology with statistical data, to create a map of multiple devices being linked to a single anonymous user. As a result, behavior of a user across their multiple devices is linked to a single SilverPush ID.

We are the only company that captures user touchpoints from TV-Desktop-Laptop-Tablets-Smartphone and make them available to our valuable customers through our data platform.

Biggest players like Google, P&G,GE and Philips uses our platform to get better understanding of their audience and reach them effectively.
